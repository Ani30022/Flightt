Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about your flight.

Booking & Tickets

How do I check in online?

Web check-in opens 48h before departure. Visit SpiceJet website, enter PNR (EIYDKX) and name, select seat, download boarding pass.

Can I modify my booking?

Yes, through airline website or customer support. Charges may apply based on fare rules.

What's the baggage allowance?

Check-in: 15kg per passenger. Cabin: 7kg. Additional baggage can be purchased.

When should I reach the airport?

Arrive 2h before departure. For 05:55 AM flight, reach by 03:55 AM. Gate closes 25 min before.

What documents do I need?

Valid govt photo ID (Aadhaar/PAN/Passport/Voter ID/DL), boarding pass, Inner Line Permit for Leh if needed.

Is insurance included?

Basic insurance may be included. Check booking details. Additional coverage available.

What if flight is delayed?

You'll be notified via SMS/email. SpiceJet will rebook or refund per policy. Contact support.

Can I select my seat?

Yes, during web check-in (free) or purchase in advance. Seat 25E already assigned.
